About

An immersive open-world cyberpunk RPG with deep character customization and branching storylines. Set in the sprawling metropolis of Neo-Tokyo 2077, players navigate through a complex web of corporate intrigue, street-level conflicts, and personal vendettas.

Featuring a revolutionary neural implant system, players can customize their character's abilities and augmentations to create unique playstyles. Every choice matters, affecting not only the story but the very fabric of the city itself.
